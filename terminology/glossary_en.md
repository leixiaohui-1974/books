## Style Guidelines for English Publications

### Capitalization Rules
- CHS-specific terms use Title Case at first mention: "Water Network Autonomy Levels (WNAL)"
- After first mention, abbreviations may be used: "WNAL L3"
- Generic technical terms use standard capitalization: "model predictive control" (lowercase) when not referring to CHS-specific usage

### Citation Format for CHS Papers
- Lei, X. et al. (2025a) → DOI: 10.13476/j.cnki.nsbdqk.2025.0077
- Lei, X. et al. (2025b) → DOI: 10.13476/j.cnki.nsbdqk.2025.0079
- Lei, X. et al. (2025c) → DOI: 10.13476/j.cnki.nsbdqk.2025.0080
- Lei, X. et al. (2025d) → DOI: 10.13476/j.cnki.nsbdqk.2025.0078

### British vs. American English
- Default: **American English** (per Springer/CRC Press style)
- Exception: IAHR publications may use British English per their style guide
